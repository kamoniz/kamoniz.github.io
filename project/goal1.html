Good Health and Well Being (UN Sustainability Goal Three)
Like this cheesy slogan informs, if you are healthy, you can live life better
Caption Three
❮ ❯

According to Medical Health Today, “Health can be defined as physical, mental, and social wellbeing, and as a resource for living a full life.” The United Nations focuses this goal on eliminating such diseases as AIDS, and malaria, but this goal is easily applied to improving our wellbeing in Canada as well. Eating healthy and getting exercise are great ways to improve your health and well being. Parks provide places for us to have a picnic, go for a walk, or play a game. Parks and greenspaces are instrumental to our health and wellbeing.
