According to the United Nations Development Program, by 2050, our world will likely have around 6.5 billion people living in urban areas. This means that it is crucial to have sufficient cities that are able to sustain its citizens effectively and with quality. Cities need to be equipped to handle larger populations while also improving the quality of life for its citizens.. This means that our cities need to make substantial investments in affordable housing, public transport, urban planning and (quite importantly) green spaces.
